Account Deletion Request

Submit a request to permanently delete your Genius App account and all associated personal data from our systems.

How the deletion process works

Once you submit your request, our team will verify the email address against our records and begin the deletion process. Here is what to expect at each stage:

  1. 1
    Request received

    We log your deletion request and send an initial confirmation to your registered email address within a few minutes of submission.

  2. 2
    Identity verification

    We verify that the email address you provide matches an active account in our system. If no match is found, we will notify you so you can resubmit with the correct address.

  3. 3
    Account deletion within 15 days

    Your account and all associated personal data will be permanently deleted from our systems within 15 calendar days of your verified request. You will receive an email confirmation once the deletion is complete.

  4. 4
    Data removed from all systems

    Upon completion, all personal information - including your account profile, usage data, and support history - will be permanently removed from our servers and from any third-party service providers we share data with in order to operate the Service.

What is not affected by account deletion

Deleting your account does not affect your self-custodial wallet or your Digital Assets. Your private keys and seed phrase remain solely in your control. Additionally, transaction data recorded on public blockchains is immutable and cannot be removed - this is an inherent property of blockchain technology outside our control. We are also required by law to retain certain transaction records for a minimum of five years for regulatory compliance purposes.

You will receive progress updates

We will keep you informed at each stage of the process via the email address associated with your account. Check your inbox - and your spam folder - for updates from us. If you have questions at any point, contact us at info@rampatm.com.
