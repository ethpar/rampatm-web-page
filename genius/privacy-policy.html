1. Overview & Scope

This Privacy Policy ("Policy") describes how Genius App, operated by RampATM Merchant Services, Inc. (rampatm.com) ("we," "us," or "our"), collects, uses, stores, and protects information in connection with your use of the Genius App mobile application, web interface, and related services (collectively, the "Service"). By accessing or using the Service, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by this Policy.

Genius App is a self-custodial digital wallet. This means we do not hold, control, or have access to your private keys or funds at any time. Our privacy practices reflect this architecture - your assets remain under your sole control, and our data collection is strictly limited to what is necessary to provide and improve the Service.

Applicability

This Policy applies to all users of Genius App globally. Where local laws impose stricter requirements, those requirements apply to the extent they are applicable to your jurisdiction.

This Policy does not apply to third-party websites or services that may be linked from within our application. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party services you access.

5. Data Storage & Security

All user data collected by Genius App is stored on servers physically located within the United States. We do not transfer or replicate personal data to data centers located outside of the United States as part of our standard operations.

We implement a layered security architecture designed to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Our security measures include:

  • Encryption at Rest: All data stored in our databases is encrypted using AES-256 encryption.
  • Encryption in Transit: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is protected by TLS 1.2 or higher.
  • Access Controls: Access to user data is restricted on a strict need-to-know basis with role-based access control. All access is logged and audited.
  • Infrastructure Security: Our servers are hosted in SOC 2 Type II certified facilities with physical security controls, redundant systems, and continuous monitoring.
  • Vulnerability Management: We conduct regular security assessments, penetration testing, and patch management procedures.
  • Incident Response: We maintain a formal incident response plan. In the event of a data breach affecting your personal information, we will notify affected users and relevant authorities as required by applicable law.

No System Is Infallible

While we implement industry-standard security measures, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, but we are committed to implementing and maintaining appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data.

7. Blockchain Transactions & On-Chain Data

Genius App is built on public blockchain infrastructure. It is important to understand the fundamental privacy implications of blockchain technology as they apply to your use of the Service.

Public Blockchain Transparency

When you initiate a transaction through Genius App, that transaction is broadcast to and permanently recorded on a public blockchain. Transaction data - including sending and receiving wallet addresses, amounts, and timestamps - is publicly visible to anyone on the network and is immutable. This is an inherent property of blockchain technology, not a practice of Genius App.

In addition to on-chain records, Genius App maintains its own internal transaction records associated with your account. These records serve the following purposes:

  • Transaction History: To provide you with an accurate and accessible history of all transactions conducted through your Genius App wallet.
  • Dispute Resolution: To assist in the investigation and resolution of any disputes, errors, or support inquiries related to transactions.
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable financial recordkeeping obligations where legally required.
  • Security Monitoring: To detect and investigate suspicious or fraudulent transaction patterns.

Our internal transaction records are stored on our secure U.S.-based servers and are protected by the security measures described in Section 5. The corresponding on-chain records are outside our control and are permanently visible on the public blockchain regardless of any action we take.

Wallet addresses you use are pseudonymous by nature. However, if your wallet address becomes associated with your identity through the use of our Service or through external means, the public transaction history associated with that address may become linkable to you.

8. Self-Custody, KYC & ATM Usage

Self-Custodial Architecture

Genius App is a self-custodial wallet. Your private keys and seed phrase are generated on your device and remain under your sole control at all times. We never transmit, store, or have access to your private keys or seed phrase. You are the sole custodian of your assets.

No KYC for Standard Wallet Use

For standard use of the Genius App wallet - including creating an account, sending and receiving digital assets, and managing your portfolio - we do not require or collect any Know Your Customer (KYC) documentation. You are not required to provide government-issued identification, proof of address, financial statements, or any other identity verification documents to use the core features of the Service.

ATM Usage & Automatic KYC

KYC Is Required for ATM Transactions

When you use a Genius App-compatible ATM and complete a transaction using a debit card, Know Your Customer verification is automatically attached to that transaction by the ATM network and the issuing bank. This is a legal requirement for card-based fiat-to-crypto transactions and operates independently of our no-KYC policy for standard wallet use.

When you conduct an ATM transaction using a debit card:

  • Your debit card details are processed by the card payment network (e.g., Visa, Mastercard) and your issuing bank, both of which are subject to their own privacy policies and KYC/AML obligations.
  • The ATM operator is required by law to record and verify the identity associated with the debit card used in the transaction.
  • A tokenized reference to your debit card transaction (not your full card number) and the ATM session details are associated with your Genius App account for recordkeeping and fraud prevention purposes.
  • The data collected at the ATM is governed by the privacy policies of the ATM operator, card network, and issuing bank, in addition to this Policy.

If you do not wish to have KYC data associated with your transactions, use only peer-to-peer on-chain transfers and do not use the ATM cash-in/cash-out functionality.

10. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this Policy, to maintain your account, and to comply with our legal obligations. The following general retention periods apply:

  • Account Data: Retained for the duration of your active account plus 90 days following account deletion, to allow for account recovery. After this period, account data is permanently deleted or irreversibly anonymized.
  • Transaction Records: Retained for a minimum of five (5) years from the date of the transaction to comply with applicable financial recordkeeping laws. This applies to our internal records; on-chain transaction data is permanent and outside our control.
  • Support Communications: Retained for two (2) years from the date of the last communication in a support thread, then permanently deleted.
  • Security Logs: Retained for 12 months for security monitoring and incident response purposes.
  • ATM Session Data: Retained in accordance with applicable financial regulations and card network rules, typically a minimum of five (5) years.

When data is no longer needed, it is securely deleted using industry-standard data destruction procedures or irreversibly anonymized so that it can no longer be associated with an individual.

To exercise any of these rights, please submit a request to info@rampatm.com. We will respond to all verifiable requests within 30 days. We may need to verify your identity before processing your request to protect the security of your account.

Limitation on Blockchain Data

The right to deletion does not extend to data recorded on a public blockchain. Blockchain transactions are immutable by design and cannot be altered or erased once confirmed. We have no technical ability to modify public blockchain records regardless of any request.
