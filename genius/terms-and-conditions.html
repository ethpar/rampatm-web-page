3. The Self-Custodial Wallet

Your Keys. Your Responsibility. Your Assets.

Genius App is a non-custodial service. We do not hold, manage, or have access to your private keys, seed phrase, or Digital Assets at any time. You are the sole custodian of your wallet and the assets within it.

What Self-Custody Means

When you create a wallet through Genius App, a cryptographic key pair is generated directly on your device. Your private key and seed phrase are derived and stored locally - they are never transmitted to our servers or held by us in any form. This architecture gives you complete sovereignty over your Digital Assets.

Critical Responsibilities for Self-Custody

The self-custodial model carries significant personal responsibility. You acknowledge and accept the following:

  • Seed Phrase is Your Only Backup: Your seed phrase is the only way to recover your wallet if your device is lost, damaged, or reset. You must write it down and store it in a safe, offline location. Do not store it digitally or share it with anyone.
  • Loss of Seed Phrase Means Permanent Loss of Funds: If you lose your seed phrase and cannot access your device, your Digital Assets are permanently and irretrievably lost. Genius App has no mechanism to recover your wallet or your funds under any circumstances.
  • We Cannot Reverse Transactions: All blockchain transactions are final and irreversible once confirmed. We have no ability to cancel, reverse, or modify any transaction after it has been broadcast to the network.
  • We Cannot Access Your Funds: We cannot freeze, move, recover, or interact with your Digital Assets in any way. This is by design.

Never Share Your Seed Phrase

Genius App employees, support staff, and contractors will never ask for your seed phrase or private key under any circumstances. Anyone asking for these credentials is attempting to steal your funds. If you share your seed phrase with anyone, you may permanently and irrecoverably lose all Digital Assets in the associated wallet.

6. Transactions & Blockchain

Transactions Are Final

All blockchain transactions initiated through Genius App are irreversible once broadcast and confirmed on the network. We have no ability to cancel, reverse, refund, or modify any transaction for any reason once it has been submitted to the blockchain.

You acknowledge and accept the following with respect to all transactions conducted through the Service:

  • Transaction Finality: Blockchain transactions are permanent and cannot be undone. Always verify the recipient address and amount before confirming. Errors, including sending to an incorrect address or wrong network, are final.
  • Network Fees: All blockchain transactions are subject to network fees (commonly called "gas fees") paid directly to network validators. These fees are not collected by Genius App, are non-refundable, and may vary significantly based on network conditions at the time of the transaction.
  • Network Congestion: Transactions may be delayed during periods of high network activity. We are not responsible for delays caused by blockchain network conditions.
  • Correct Network Selection: You are solely responsible for ensuring that you are transacting on the correct blockchain network. Sending assets to an address on the wrong network may result in permanent, irrecoverable loss of those assets.
  • Smart Contract Interactions: Interactions with smart contracts carry additional risks, including the possibility of bugs, exploits, or unexpected outcomes in the smart contract code. We do not audit or endorse any smart contracts, and you interact with them entirely at your own risk.
  • Volatility: The value of Digital Assets is extremely volatile. Genius App makes no representation regarding the current or future value of any Digital Asset and is not liable for any losses arising from price fluctuations.
